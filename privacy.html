Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

YourBloom Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website yourbloom.site or purchase our Notion operating systems, AI automations, or consulting services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out contact or order forms (name, email, business details)
  • Purchase a service (payment details processed securely via Stripe)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or download a free template
  • Contact us via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referral URLs. This data is used for analytics, security, and improving user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver, customize, and support your Notion system or AI automation
  • To process payments and manage orders
  • To communicate about your project, updates, or support requests
  • To send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent)
  • To improve our services, website functionality, and client experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, under strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Stripe — secure payment processing
  • Notion — hosting and delivering your workspace
  • Zapier / Make — automation integrations
  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Email service providers — for newsletters and project updates

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and secure networks when accessing your accounts.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

California, Colorado, Virginia, and other US state residents have additional rights under state privacy laws. We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typically, client data is retained for 3 years after the last interaction, unless otherwise required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of underage data collection, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
