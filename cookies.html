Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how YourBloom Studio ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website yourbloom.site.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide insights to site owners about how their site is used.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality (e.g., navigation, secure areas)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices (e.g., language, region)
  • Marketing cookies: Track your activity across sites to deliver relevant content (we do not currently use third-party advertising cookies)

3. Cookies We Use

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties to place cookies on your device through our website:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Notion: For delivering your workspace. Notion Privacy Policy

These third parties have their own cookie policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. How to Control Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Disabling analytics cookies helps us improve less effectively.

6. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature. Because there is no uniform industry standard for responding to DNT signals, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals. If a universal standard is adopted, we will update this policy accordingly.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
